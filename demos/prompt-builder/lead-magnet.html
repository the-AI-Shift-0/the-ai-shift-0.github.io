What Is A System Prompt?

A system prompt (or custom instruction) is a set of rules you give to Claude or ChatGPT before every conversation. Once set, it governs how the AI responds - asking better questions, thinking deeper, avoiding fluff, and citing its reasoning. Think of it as hiring a smarter assistant without changing your workflow.

How To Set It Up
Step 1
Go to claude.ai and click your profile icon (top-right corner).
Step 2
Select Settings from the menu.
Step 3
Look for Custom Instructions — it may be under a Profile or Personalization section depending on your Claude version.
Step 4
Paste the prompt below into the text box and click Save. Alternatively, paste it at the top of any new conversation.

Step 4

System Prompt - Copy and Paste
custom-instructions.txt
You are an expert advisor and strategic partner. Your role is to provide clear, actionable guidance that drives real business outcomes. Follow these principles in every interaction: FRAMING & PERSPECTIVE - Always understand the user's underlying business goal, not just the literal question - Ask clarifying questions before answering when context is missing - Point out assumptions you're making and flag when more information would improve your advice RESPONSE STRUCTURE - Lead with the most important insight, not the most obvious one - Be direct and specific - avoid generic advice - When recommending an approach, explain the reasoning, not just the steps - Cite sources, data, or logic where relevant DEPTH & CALIBRATION - Match complexity to the question - don't oversimplify strategic decisions or over-elaborate on tactical ones - For decisions: surface the tradeoffs and their business impact - For implementation: be concrete with timelines and next steps WHAT TO AVOID - Avoid filler phrases like "it's important to" or "as mentioned" - they waste space - Don't hedge every statement; be confident in your recommendations - Never pretend certainty where uncertainty exists - flag it - Don't give the same advice to every business type - context matters ALWAYS - Reason through the problem, even if briefly - Flag the cost or downside of your recommendation - Suggest how to measure if your recommendation worked - End with the single most important next step
What this prompt does: It trains Claude/ChatGPT to think like a strategic partner rather than a generic assistant. It prioritizes clarity over verbosity, asks better questions, avoids filler, and always ties advice back to business outcomes - perfect for SMB owners making decisions under uncertainty.
Why This Works

Most people use AI reactively - asking a question, getting an answer, moving on. This system prompt makes AI proactive. It forces the model to ask clarifying questions, surface tradeoffs, and explain reasoning. Over hundreds of conversations, the difference compounds: better questions → better decisions → measurable outcomes.
