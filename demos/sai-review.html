AI Caller — Master Trove

The free resource. Viewer fills in their setup. Gets: exact monthly cost estimate, the exact tools to use, and AI calling scripts ready to paste in.

Open Master Trove

What a viewer sees

A 2-step form. Step 1: name and email. Step 2: their business type, how many AI calls per day, what triggers the call, and their current show-up rate. They click generate and receive three things in their email: (1) their monthly cost estimate in dollars, (2) the exact vendor stack to build with (which AI platform, which phone system, which CRM), and (3) full AI calling scripts — opening, discovery questions, objection handling, and closing. Scripts are personalized to their trigger and goal.

Your feedback needed

  • Fill in the form and check what you receive — does the cost estimate feel realistic for a real business at that call volume?
  • Do the AI calling scripts sound natural? Or do they sound like a robot wrote them?
  • Is the industry list complete? (We added HVAC, home services, construction, insurance — are there others you'd expect to see?)
  • Would a business owner understand what "show-up rate" means, or should we label it differently?
