And What Consultants Will Try to Charge You

The Real Stack Breakdown

Component What It Does Monthly Cost Notes
AI Voice Platform Speaks and understands responses (Retell AI, Bland AI, VAPI) $200–1,500 $0.07–0.14/min per call + subscription

Calendar Integration AI books directly into your calendar (Calendly, OnceHub, Acuity) $8–24 Usually a standard plan feature
Workflow Automation Connects CRM trigger to calling system (n8n, Make, Zapier) $20–100 Orchestrates everything behind the scenes

Backend / Hosting Runs the system 24/7 (Google Cloud Run, Railway, Render) $30–150 Minimal after initial setup
Developer Setup One-time wiring of the entire system — this is the real work $3,000–15,000 one-time, not monthly Where you get ripped off. 20–60 hrs of developer work at $100–200/hr.

What You Should Actually Pay

Month 1 (setup + first month running):

$3,500–17,000

Developer setup ($3K–15K one-time) + first month of platform costs ($350–2K).

Every month after that:

$350–2,000/month

Most $2–10M businesses land around $400–600/month depending on call volume.

The trap: Consultants charge $25,000–80,000 upfront "AI implementation fees." The ongoing tech costs $400/month. The one-time setup is 20–60 hrs of developer work — that's the only thing worth paying for.

Red Flags: Walk Away If You Hear These

"We built our own AI"

They didn't. It's Retell, Bland, or VAPI with their branding slapped on top.

"You need a 12-month contract to see results"

The system either works in week 1 or it doesn't. Contracts hide bad ROI.

Bundling call center staff with the AI

That defeats the entire purpose. You want to replace staff time, not pay for both.

"Proprietary integration fee" for Twilio or calendar

This takes 2 hours to set up. Anything more is a scam.

No performance SLA or guarantee

If they won't commit to a show-up rate or lead quality improvement, walk.

Monthly retainers above $1,500 for maintenance

Once the system is live, it should be stable. Maintenance should be minimal.

Questions to Ask Any Vendor

  • What AI voice provider do you use? (If they won't say, walk.)
  • What's the per-minute cost of calls on your platform?
  • Who owns the phone numbers — you or us?
  • What happens if we stop working with you — do we keep the system and data?
  • Can you show me a live demo on a test account with real integrations?
  • What's your expected setup time and total project cost?
  • What metrics will you track to prove this works for our business?
