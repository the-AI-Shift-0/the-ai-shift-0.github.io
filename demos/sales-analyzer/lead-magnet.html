How to load this into your AI

  1. Go to claude.ai and click Projects in the left sidebar
  2. Click New Project and give it a name (e.g. "Sales Analyzer")
  3. Click Set Project Instructions
  4. Paste your Master Trove into the instructions box — click Save
  5. Now drop any sales call transcript into the chat and type: "Analyze this call"
Every conversation in this Project automatically uses your Master Trove. One setup, unlimited calls.
